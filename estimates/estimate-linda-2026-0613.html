Project Overview
This estimate covers four (4) scopes of work at the subject property: (1) full assessment and structural repair of the settled concrete driveway slab — including foundation gap correction at the garage opening and crack remediation; (2) removal and replacement of the deteriorated brick apron at the street entrance; and (3) design and installation of a new decorative concrete-paver walkway from the driveway to the front entry door, inspired by the client's selected reference style (large-format pavers with black river rock border); and (4) installation of matching decorative paver walkways along both side yards of the property, including full cleaning, repair, and upgrade of all existing drainage grates (tragantes) to prevent water accumulation and clogging.
