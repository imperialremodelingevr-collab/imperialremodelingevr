Project Overview

This estimate covers the complete removal of all existing cabinetry throughout the residence, followed by the supply of labor and installation of approximately 21 new high-quality cabinets across five (5) areas: full kitchen, two (2) bathrooms, laundry room, and living room. All work will be performed by skilled craftsmen to the highest industry standards.
